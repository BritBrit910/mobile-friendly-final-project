About My Materials

Paints

My favorite acrylic paint to use is Folk Art Acrylic Paints, which can be purchased at Michaels, Joann, or basically any arts and crafts store.

Pencils and Pens

For all of my colored pencil drawings, my favorite kind to use are Prismacolor Colored Pencils. For regular pencil and/or pen drawings however, I do not have a favorite and just go with any type of ink pen or drawing pencil.

Body/Face Paint

The face paints I use and would Highly recommend are Wolfe Face Paints, the pigments in these paints are amazing and they do not become diluted easily like most other face paints.

Graphic Design Programs

For all of my digital artwork, I use the Adobe Creative Cloud programs Photoshop and Illustrator. For artwork that looks more detailed and hand drawn, I prefer Photoshop. For neat vector artwork, I use Illustrator.
